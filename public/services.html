Adopt a Child

Our "Adopt a Child" initiative allows individuals and organizations to sponsor the education and well-being of a child in need. By becoming a sponsor, you can make a direct and lasting impact on a child's life, providing them with the opportunity to break free from the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future.

Udaan - Empowering Education

Udaan is our flagship educational program designed to support children who face barriers to completing their education. Through Udaan, we provide academic resources, mentorship, and holistic support to help these children overcome challenges and pursue their dreams.

About Us

Shree Educational Trust, established in 2024, is a non-profit organization driven by the unwavering belief that education is the cornerstone of empowerment and social change. Our journey began with a vision to transform the lives of children from underserved communities by providing them with access to quality education and holistic support systems.

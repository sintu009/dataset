About Us:

Absolutely! Shree Educational Trust, which began in 2024, is all about making sure every child gets a chance at great education and opportunities to succeed. Our goal is to help kids from less fortunate backgrounds do their best and become leaders who can make a real difference where they live.

Our programs focus on giving these kids top-notch education, mentorship, and resources they need to thrive. We want to see them not just succeed for themselves but also to become positive forces in their communities. Whether it's through academics, arts, or sports, we encourage their passions and help them build skills that will serve them well throughout life.

By supporting us, you're not just helping individual kids; you're helping build a better future for entire communities. Together, we can make sure every child's potential is recognized and nurtured, creating a ripple effect of positive change that spreads far and wide.

Our Mission

At Shree Educational Trust, founded in 2024, we are committed to providing every child with access to quality education and opportunities for growth. Our mission is to empower children from underprivileged backgrounds to reach their full potential and become agents of change in their communities

Our Vision

At Shree Educational Trust, we envision a world where every child, regardless of their background or circumstances, has the opportunity to unlock their full potential and pursue their dreams. We aspire to create a society where education is not just a privilege but a fundamental right for every child.

Our Services

We are a non-profital & Charity raising money for child education

Udaan - Empowering Education

Udaan is our flagship educational program designed to support children who face barriers to completing their education. Through Udaan, we provide academic resources, mentorship, and holistic support to help these children overcome challenges and pursue their dreams.

Adopt a Child

Our "Adopt a Child" initiative allows individuals and organizations to sponsor the education and well-being of a child in need. By becoming a sponsor, you can make a direct and lasting impact on a child's life, providing them with the opportunity to break free from the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future.
